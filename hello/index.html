Frequently asked questions

How did this ring come about?
Development started in September 2012
Which digital door locks work with the ring?
Any NFC door lock will work however be mindful of cheep imitation door locks that don't have genuine NFC support. We recommend the Samsung SHS-2421 as a good sub 100$ digital door lock that is easy to fit.
What operating systems will be supported at Launch?
Android.
What operating systems will be supported after Launch?
We will set kickstarter targets where people can vote to get their OS supported. The app is developed with Apache Cordova(PhoneGap) so supporting additional devices should be relatively trivial.
What's the status with unlocking my phone with the ring?
You can use third party software to achieve this however third party software is somewhat limited. We will be setting Kickstarter targets for achieving this natively in Android
Why chose open source and what does it mean for the project?
A ring should be both personal and community focused, because sharing is a big part of the core functionality we needed a way to easily support third party platforms, open source allows this.
Why put two inlays in the ring?
For privacy. One inlay is private, one inlay is public. Different hand gestures mean different information is shared.
How many rings currently exist?
50 wood inlays, 1 completely clear, 100 or so prototypes (mostly 3d printed)
What can the ring be used for?
Unlocking doors, Unlocking mobile phones, sharing URLS, sharing information.
Who is behind the product?
John McLear, the founder of Primary Technology and the Etherpad Foundation and Matt Mullenweg, the founder of Wordpress(Automattik) and various other projects.

Is the inlay actual NFC and not an imitation?
Yes, we use the ntag203 chip

Roughly 7mm

Not yet
Is the data stored on the ring encrypted or secured?
Your physical security is the security, there is no encryption. It is very similar to a door key.
Can I purchase rings in bulk for my exhibition/company or so?
After Kickstarter we will be taking orders of bulk purchases of the ring
